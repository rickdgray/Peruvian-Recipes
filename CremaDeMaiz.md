# Crema de Maíz

\rcAuthorSymbol{} By
: Yilmar García

\rcClockSymbol{} Ready in
: 30 minutes

\rcServingSymbol{} Serves
: 1

## Ingredients

- 75g of fresh corn
- 10g of huacatay (Peruvian Black Mint), can be replaced with native spices.
- ¼ onion
- Salt to taste
- Egg white

> Crema de Maíz is delicious!
